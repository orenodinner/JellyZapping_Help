# Privacy Policy (English)

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**App Name:** JellyZapping

[oreno_dinner] (hereinafter referred to as "we") has established the following privacy policy regarding the handling of user information in the smartphone application "JellyZapping" (hereinafter referred to as "this App").

### 1. Information Collection and Use

#### 1.1 Jellyfin Server Credentials
This App allows you to connect to a Jellyfin media server. To function, it handles the following data:
*   **Data:** Server URL, Username, Password, Access Token.
*   **Purpose:** To authenticate and connect to your Jellyfin server for media playback.
*   **Storage & Transmission:** These credentials are stored securely on your device using the iOS Keychain. All communication occurs directly between your device and your Jellyfin server. We do not transmit or store this data on our servers.

#### 1.2 Advertising (Google AdMob)
The free version of this App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. The following information may be collected automatically:
*   **Data:** Advertising Identifier (IDFA), Device Information, Interaction data with ads, Crash logs.
*   **Purpose:** To display personalized ads, measure ad performance, and improve app stability.
*   **Third-Party:** This information is sent to Google Inc. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.
    *   [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

#### 1.3 In-App Purchases
This App uses Apple's In-App Purchase system for unlocking Pro features.
*   Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store your credit card information or billing details.

#### 1.4 Request sending function Feature
This App includes a feature to send media metadata to an external URL (Webhooks).
*   **Usage:** This feature only functions if you explicitly enter a "Destination URL" in the app settings.
*   **Data:** Metadata such as Title, ID, and File Path are sent to the URL you specify. We do not collect this data.

### 2. App Permissions

*   **Local Network:** Used to discover and connect to Jellyfin servers located on your local network.
*   **Tracking (ATT):** We may request permission to track your activity across apps and websites to deliver personalized ads. You can change this preference in your iOS settings at any time.

### 3. Data Security
We do not intentionally collect or store your personal information on our servers (excluding anonymous data collected by AdMob). Authentication credentials stored within the App remain solely on your device.

### 4. Disclaimer
This App is designed to connect to servers managed by the user or servers to which the user has authorized access. Users are responsible for the security settings of the connected servers and the rights regarding the content on those servers.

### 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page or within the App.
